Geometry¶

Let the slope have a vertical height $H$ and the slope angle $\beta$. The geometry of the slope is then given by:

  • Foot: $F = [0,0]$
  • Shoulder: $S = [H / \tan \beta, H]$

Assumptions / Limitations¶

  • Dry soil
  • Slip circle through foot
